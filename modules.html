Marketing

In the context of growing market competition, organizations are focusing more on closer partnership across supply chain. Increasing efficiency in market and distribution process facilitates an organization to maintain its competitive edge. KeaGMP enable the client to channelize their efforts in reaching their customers by providing best possible solutions

Warehouse

Defined as a key part of the supply chain and primarily aims to control the movement and storage of materials within a warehouse and process the associated transactions, including shipping, receiving, put away and picking. KeaGMP tracks every item in warehouse individual /group level enabling the client to maintain the stock at optimum levels.

QC

Facilities are versatile and offer support for every phase of client’s operations, from receipt and acceptance of raw materials, through production of intermediates, to inspections of finished goods before shipment to customers. KeaGMP ensure the quality checks at all critical points as per the Industry’s best practices.

QA

KeaGMP facilitates client in preventing mistakes and defects in manufactured products and avoiding problems when delivering products and services to their customers. This not only enhances the corporate profitability but also facilitate in faster penetration over their peers in the market.

Engineering

Is designed to render a powerful advantage for the operation and planning of business units. Engineering supports planning resources for engineered parts, services. KeaGMP enables the client to exercise the best methods in maintaining the internal engineering related services and facilitates to adopt a suitable maintenance services for their Plant and machinery.

Production

Is the process by which raw materials are transformed into finished products. KeaGMP enable the client in obtaining the relevant BPR (batch process records) from the QA and managing the typical indenting, product analysis and complete the production process as per the scheduled time.

SCM

KeaGMP facilitate to handle the entire supply chain management (SCM), from start to finish. SCM modules typically include components for forecasting, demand management, procurement and planning, delivery modules such as logistics and components for after-market issues like returns, installations and contracts.

Accounts

Is provides companywide control and integration of financial information. This module provides the ability to centrally track financial accounting data within a framework of multiple companies, languages and currencies.

HRMS

KeaGMP facilitate the client in managing their human resources related services viz., employee master, training, skill set management, payslip, leave management and performance assessment exercises etc. This is an integral part of core system.
